About Director Kinn

Born in 1998 into a multicultural family, Kidus Sirak's visual journey began with a childhood immersed in old movies and music. A self-taught artist, he launched his creative career early as a photographer and graphic designer. In 2019, driven by a lack of opportunity, he independently produced and directed his first music video, a pivotal moment that solidified his life's calling.

Kidus's expertise stems from hands-on experience and continuous online learning, nurtured through "Bete Adwa Studio," a group he co-founded. His professional dancing background provides a unique understanding of rhythm and visuals, leading to collaborations with Ethiopia's top artists over six dynamic years.

Today, Kidus channels his vision through Kinn Films, a production house guided by the ethos: "See where imagination takes us."
